# Third Party Notices

This package bundles no third-party code or assets. The runtime depends only on Unity engine
modules (Animation, Playables) and the editor tooling on the Unity Editor APIs.

The "Locomotion Demo" sample requires a user-supplied Humanoid or Generic rig. If none is provided,
the sample generates its own placeholder locomotion clips procedurally; no external animation data
is included or downloaded.

Characters and animations obtained from third-party services (for example Adobe Mixamo) remain the
property of their respective owners and are subject to their own licenses. They are not part of, and
are not redistributed by, this package.
